Samoyed

Samoyed has a friendly, good-natured disposition and is especially fond of children.

  • Weight: 50 to 60 pounds
  • Average Life Span: 12-15 years
  • Amazing fact: The Samoyed breed is one of 14 ancient dog breeds with genetic footprints closest to wolves.
  • Energy Level: High Exercise Needs / High Potential for Playfulness

The hardy and sweet-tempered Samoyed was originally used to hunt, herd reindeer, and haul sledges for the Siberian Samoyede people. His white, fluffy coat is beautiful, but requires rigorous grooming. His reputation as an intelligent family dog is well deserved. He is happiest when he is part of family life, and needs to be with people.
